Export-Safe Packaging

🛡️ Our Breakage Rate: < 0.5% — Industry average is 2-3%

Glass requires specialized packaging for international shipping. We use a 3-layer protection system:

Layer 1: Individual Wrap

Each piece wrapped in bubble wrap + foam sleeve. Separators prevent contact.

Layer 2: Inner Carton

Corrugated dividers create individual cells. Shock-absorbing corner protectors.

Layer 3: Master Carton

Double-wall 5-ply carton. Palletized for container loading. Fumigation certificate available.

Insurance: We can arrange cargo insurance covering breakage during transit.

Glass-Specific Quality Checks

1. Visual Inspection

Check for bubbles, cracks, chips, and inclusions. Light transmission test for clarity.

2. Dimensional Accuracy

Height, diameter, and wall thickness measured against specifications. Weight consistency check.

3. Packaging Stress Test

Drop test from 1.2m height. Vibration test simulating container transport. Corner crush test.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do you prevent breakage during shipping?

Our 3-layer packaging system + palletized loading results in < 0.5% breakage rate. We also offer cargo insurance for full protection.

Q: Can you match a specific glass color I have?

Yes. Send us a physical sample or Pantone code. We'll create color samples for your approval before production.

Q: What's the difference between hand-blown and machine-made?

Hand-blown: Unique variations, premium feel, higher MOQ (200pcs). Machine-made: Consistent, cost-effective, faster production. We recommend machine-made for volume orders.

Q: Can I combine glass and ceramic in one order?

Absolutely. Many clients do this to optimize container space. We handle mixed QC and coordinated shipping.
