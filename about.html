Our Story

Founded in 2014, KJadeHome brings over 15 years of experience in product development, factory management, and product compliance.

We don't just manufacture — we ensure every shipment is on time and meets customer standards. Our network of 10+ partnership factories gives us the flexibility to handle diverse product categories while maintaining consistent quality.

From ceramic and glass decor to pet products, we've grown alongside our clients. When they succeed, we succeed.
