Our Story

KJadeHome began in 2011 as a small ceramic workshop serving European importers. Over 15 years, we've grown into a comprehensive OEM manufacturer with capabilities across ceramics, glass, and pet products.

What hasn't changed is our commitment to quality and reliability. We understand that our success depends on our clients' success — when your products sell well, you reorder. It's that simple.

Today, we serve retailers and importers across the US, Europe, Australia, and Southeast Asia. Many of our clients have been with us for 5+ years, a testament to the consistency they've come to expect.
