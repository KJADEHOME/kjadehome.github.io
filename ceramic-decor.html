Our 3-Step Quality System

1. Pre-Production

Raw material inspection and glaze color confirmation. We test fire samples to ensure color accuracy.

2. In-Line Inspection

Daily factory checks during production. Dimensional accuracy, glaze consistency, and visual defect screening.

3. Final QC

100% visual inspection + random sampling for drop tests and packaging stress tests. AQL 2.5 standard.

Defect rate: < 0.3% | Third-party inspection: Available on request (SGS, Bureau Veritas)

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can you replicate my existing ceramic product?

Yes. Send us photos, samples, or technical drawings. Our engineers will assess feasibility and provide tooling costs within 48 hours.

Q: What if the sample color doesn't match my requirement?

We offer up to 2 rounds of free color revisions. For complex glazes, we recommend ordering a small test batch before full production.

Q: Do you hold stock for reorders?

Yes. For ongoing partnerships, we offer a stocking program where we maintain 1-3 months inventory for fast replenishment.

Q: Can I visit the factory?

Absolutely. We welcome factory visits with prior arrangement. We can also arrange video calls for virtual tours.
