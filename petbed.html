Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can you make orthopedic beds for large breeds (50kg+ dogs)?

Yes. We use high-density foam (50D+) and reinforced construction. Maximum recommended size: 120x100cm with 6" foam thickness.

Q: Are the materials safe if my dog chews the bed?

We use non-toxic, food-grade materials. However, no bed is indestructible. We offer chew-resistant fabric options for heavy chewers.

Q: Can I get samples of different foam densities?

Absolutely. We recommend ordering a sample kit with 3 foam densities (soft/medium/firm) to test with your target pet size.

Q: How does compression packing work?

Beds are vacuum-compressed to reduce shipping volume by ~70%. They recover original shape within 24-48 hours after opening. No quality loss.

Q: Can you do drop-shipping for my online store?

Yes. We can ship directly to your customers with your branding (blind shipping). No KJadeHome branding on package.
