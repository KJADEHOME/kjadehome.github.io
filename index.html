Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for custom products?

Our MOQ starts at 100 pieces per design for most ceramic and glass decor items. For pet products, MOQ varies by product type. Contact us for specific product MOQ details.

How long does production take for OEM orders?

Standard production time is 30-45 days after sample approval. Expedited production options are available for urgent orders.

What shipping terms do you offer?

We offer FOB, CIF, and DDP shipping options. Our logistics team handles customs documentation and compliance labels for smooth delivery.

Are you a BSCI certified factory?

Yes, our factory is BSCI certified, ensuring ethical manufacturing practices and compliance with international labor standards.

Do you provide samples before bulk production?

Yes, we provide samples within 7 days of request. Sample revisions are free until you approve the final design.

What countries do you export to?

We export to the United States, Europe, Australia, and ASEAN countries. We have been serving international retailers and importers since 2011.
